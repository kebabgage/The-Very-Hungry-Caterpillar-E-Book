About the Author

Eric Carle (age 88) is an American designer, illistrator and author. Since the The Very Hungry Caterpillar was published, he has illistrated more than 70 books. Carle has a son and a daughter. Eric's wife founded The Eric Carle Museum of Picture Book art that is devoted to the art of children's books.
